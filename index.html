SimplyRuthyDIY is an online boutiqe dedicated to creating fashion products for medical professionals.

Introducing, SimplyRuthyDIY!

Hi, I'm Ruth! I spent eight years serving in the United States Army. Now, I am dedicating my life to help heal the sick and solve disparities in the healthcare fashion industry. Working 10 years in the healthcare field has brought to my attention the lack of health care fashion available to professionals working in all areas of health care. This has motivated me to create vibrant, creative, and inspiring clothing and accessories for all health care professionals.

SimplyRuthyDIY is a woman and veteran owned small business by Ruth Santana.
