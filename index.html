Pia Campbell:
Creative Marketing Manager

Hi, I'm Pia!

I am a digital marketer and content creator with three years of experience. I am always on the lookout for new opportunities to develop my skills and take on new challenges. When I'm not working, you can find me strolling through the botanical gardens, trying out new restaurants, and listening to music. Currently, I am a creative marketing manager for Saucehouse BBQ, The Bar, and The Venue on Broad. My skills in data analysis, problem-solving, and communication have been instrumental in driving the success of these businesses. Prior to this, I worked as a social media manager for Urban Sanctuary and as a social media coordinator for Athens Farmers Market. I am a graduate of Georgia Southwestern State University's Marketing program, where I learned to hone my skills and develop my expertise in the field. I am excited about the possibility of joining a new team and bringing my passion and knowledge to the table. Let's work together to create something great!
